Privacy Policy for Weather Forecast App

Last updated: [Date]

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information to provide weather services:

  • Location Data: Only when you use "My Location" feature. We never store your precise location.
  • City Names: When you manually enter or save favorite locations.
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics for crash reporting and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide accurate weather forecasts
  • Save your favorite locations locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and fix errors

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:

  • Favorite locations are saved using AsyncStorage
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • You can delete all data anytime using "Clear Favorites"

4. Third-Party Services

We use OpenWeatherMap API to provide weather data. When you request weather information:

  • City names or coordinates are sent to their API
  • We don't control their privacy practices (view their policy at openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy)

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
